Sports

Sports are physical activities that involve skill, competition, and physical exertion. They are performed individually or in teams and have been a central part of human society for centuries.
Sports such as soccer, basketball, and tennis provide not only entertainment but also physical benefits, enhancing health and fitness. They play an important role in building character, teamwork, and leadership skills.
The earliest known sports were part of ancient rituals and were often used to train warriors. Over time, these activities evolved into the organized sports we know today, with competitions like the Olympics and the World Cup becoming major global events that unite people from all cultures.
